Home dashboard help

Hello! When you first login to SuccessKPI, you will be placed in the Home dashboard under the Home area of the product. The Home dashboard is set of reports, set up by your administrator or analyst, which is customized for your role and work group. Here you can get quick “at-a-glance” information, with the metrics and visualizations that are most relevant to help you monitor your contact center. This dashboard can be customized for your role, or for you as an individual, depending upon your administrator and your preferences. To navigate, here are a few tips :

Within the initial Home Dashboard

The product is shipped with an initial Home dashboard that contains a word cloud of top key phrases, a sentiment trend, top topics, IVR and ACD Interaction counts, AHT in seconds and the AHT trend by day in a bar chart. If you have not made any calls, there may not be data yet in your setup. Please wait a few hours and make some test calls to ensure data is flowing through from your contact center to the visualizations. This dashboard can be easily configured and customized to your preferences in the Customize dashboard settings or new visualizations can be selected from the Analytics tab. From this dossier, you can explore the library, you can examine the dossier or return to the main dossier by clicking on Home dashboard again. If you make changes to the dossier, you can hit the “reset” clock icon at the top to return to initial settings. You can also change the time period by clicking on the “?” re-prompt. You can share this dossier by pdf or to other users by clicking file and then share in the top left of the dossier.

Home tab Side Navigation

There are three tabs here including Home dashboard where you begin, Customize dashboard, and this Help screen.

The Customize dashboard tab

Clicking on the Customize dashboard tab will bring you to advanced configurations settings within the dossier editing tool available only to administrators and analysts. Here you can change naming conventions, reports, data sets, and customize the appearance of your data visualizations that appear for users in the Home dashboard tab.

Top navigation

To help with basic orientation, the top navigation bar will guide you to the major areas of the product including Home, Analytics, Evaluations, Playbooks and Settings:

Home: This is the initial starting place where you arrive upon login.

Analytics: Here you will find the “Key Questions” entry page. If you are an analyst or an administrator you will also have access to the Shared Dashboards library, Custom Reports, and the Subscriptions tab.

Evaluations: This tab is available to supervisors, analysts, and administrators and give quality control teams the ability to creates scorecards, to select calls for evaluation, and to listen to calls, view transcripts, and score individual calls according the custom scorecards.

Playbooks: Typically used by analysts and administrators, the Playbooks section provides access to the speech and text analytics configurations as well as the ability to build active playbooks to take action on scenarios set by your team.
